About Us

Heri Wema is a Non-Governmental Organization basic health centre located in Ruai (Off Kangundo Road To Sewage Opposite Nairobi Water) Kasarani in Nairobi County. As of 2021, the facility was fully operational, regulated by Kenya Medical Practitioners and Dentists Council under registration number 21295. You can reach Heri Wema Health Centre (Ruai) by call or text through our number.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions to help you navigate our services easily.

What services do you offer?

We offer a range of services including general healthcare, specialized consultations, preventive care, and wellness programs.

How can I make an appointment?

Appointments can be made online through our website or by calling our office directly during business hours.

Can I request my medical records?

Yes, you can request your medical records by filling out a release form in our office or online.

What are your office hours?

Our office hours are Monday to Friday, 8 AM to 5 PM. We are open on Saturdays from 9 AM to 1 PM and closed on Sundays.

What should I do in case of a medical emergency?

If you have a medical emergency, please call 911 or go to the nearest emergency room immediately.

Are walk-in appointments available?

We primarily operate on an appointment basis, but we do accommodate urgent walk-in cases when possible.
